Key Features

Private

After first launch, all features work offline, and all processing happens on your device. No data ever leaves your computer.

Fast

Voice commands trigger in less than 200ms from when you stop speaking, which is much faster than other voice assistants.

Predictive

Vocalance learns from your usage patterns to predict your next command and execute it before you finish speaking.

Free

Vocalance is, and always will be, 100% free to use. All code is open source and public.
